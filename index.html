Privacy summary

What this page does: When you load this page, it calls a public IP‑lookup service (ipinfo.io) from your browser to retrieve your public IP, approximate city/region/country, and ISP name. Your browser and device type are parsed locally from your own user‑agent string.

What this page does not do: It does not send your data to Microsoft servers, does not store it, does not use cookies or analytics, and does not automatically start any download or remote session. The redirect to UltraViewer only happens if you tick the consent box and click the button yourself.

Third parties: If you continue, UltraViewer's own privacy policy and terms apply to your use of their software.
